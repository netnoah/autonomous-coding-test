🧪 Player Directional Facing Feature Test

Quick Start

🎮 Open Game (Port 3001)

What to test: Verify that player sprite changes direction based on movement

Direction Mapping

Movement Keys Expected Sprite
Move UP ↑ Arrow or W ⬆️
Move DOWN ↓ Arrow or S ⬇️
Move LEFT ← Arrow or A ⬅️
Move RIGHT → Arrow or D ➡️

Test Steps

Test 1: Initial Direction

Step 1: Open game and click "New Game"
Expected: Player sprite shows ⬇️ (facing down, default)

Test 2: Arrow Keys Direction

Step 1: Press Up Arrow (↑)
Expected: Sprite changes to ⬆️, player moves up
Step 2: Press Down Arrow (↓)
Expected: Sprite changes to ⬇️, player moves down
Step 3: Press Left Arrow (←)
Expected: Sprite changes to ⬅️, player moves left
Step 4: Press Right Arrow (→)
Expected: Sprite changes to ➡️, player moves right

Test 3: WASD Keys Direction

Step 1: Press W key
Expected: Sprite shows ⬆️
Step 2: Press S key
Expected: Sprite shows ⬇️
Step 3: Press A key
Expected: Sprite shows ⬅️
Step 4: Press D key
Expected: Sprite shows ➡️

Test 4: Mouse Click Direction

Step 1: Click tile above player
Expected: Sprite shows ⬆️
Step 2: Click tile below player
Expected: Sprite shows ⬇️
Step 3: Click tile left of player
Expected: Sprite shows ⬅️
Step 4: Click tile right of player
Expected: Sprite shows ➡️

Test 5: Direction Persists Through Actions

Step 1: Move to pick up an item (e.g., potion)
Expected: Direction remains from movement direction after pickup
Step 2: Move into a monster to attack
Expected: Direction shows direction you attacked from
Step 3: Move through a door
Expected: Direction shows direction you moved through door
